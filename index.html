About Me

I’m Greg Rose, an expert in Eastern Dance, with over a decade of experience in teaching, performing, and choreography. I’ve traveled the world to bring the beauty of Eastern movement to audiences of all ages and have a passion for helping others express themselves through dance.

Whether on stage, in the studio, or teaching aspiring dancers, I believe dance is a universal language that connects the soul to the physical world. Join me in exploring this wonderful journey of expression and self-discovery!
